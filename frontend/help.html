Help & Support

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I share files with others?

Select the file in your dashboard and click "Share". You can generate a temporary link for sharing.

What is the difference between Academic and Personal mode?

Academic mode is structured for assignments and study materials. Personal mode is for general files like photos and documents.

How much storage do I have?

By default, every user gets 15 GB of free storage. Check your dashboard for usage details.
